Who We Are

Precision Translations of Minnesota LLC is a professional translation organization with over 15 years of experience in helping the Immigrant Community as well as businesses and individuals. We provide translation services for birth certificates, marriage certificates, divorce decrees, death certificates, police reports, adoption agreements, school transcripts, diplomas and all types of legal, technical and medical documents. Our Company is your number one source for professional translation services; we offer certified, high-quality translation services at reasonable prices. Customer satisfaction guaranteed. Notarization and Certification services available.

Nuestra empresa es una organizacion que ofrece servicios de traducciones de alta calidad y profesionalismo, tenemos mas de 15 años de experiencia brindando servicios de calidad a la comunidad inmigrante y a organizaciones o personas naturales. Hacemos traducciones de Español a Ingles y de Ingles a Español, somos especialistas en documentos legales como actas de nacimiento, matrimonio, divorcio, defuncion, antecedentes judiciales, informes policiales, transcripciones escolares, diplomas y cualquier tipo de documento legal, medico o técnico. Ofrecemos traducciones certificadas y notarizadas de alta calidad a precios comodos. Satisfaccion del cliente es garantizada.
